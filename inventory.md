## Slot reference

| Slot | Notes |
|---|---|
| Head | Circlets, hoods, helmets — usually +Mind/Wisdom |
| Chest | Armor, robes, tunics — usually +HP or +Vitality |
| Hands | Gauntlets, gloves — usually +Strength or +Craft |
| Feet | Boots, slippers — usually +Endurance or +Agility |
| Weapon | Swords, bows, staves, lutes — theme-appropriate, big bonuses |
| Relic | Rings, pendants, charms — usually flat multipliers |

## Gear rarity colors (for reference)

- **Common** — white — flat +2 to +3 to a stat
- **Uncommon** — green — +4 to +6, sometimes a small % buff
- **Rare** — blue — multi-stat bonuses, +10-15% XP buffs
- **Epic** — purple — significant buffs, unique effects
- **Legendary** — orange — game-changing. Earned only through 30-day streaks or boss kills.
