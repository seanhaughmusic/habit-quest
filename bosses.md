# Boss Roster

Bosses appear on a schedule. Each has a **Gate** (a real-world condition you must meet to initiate the fight) and a **Power Check** (the stat total you need to win). You don't fight until the gate is open — your character is physically barred by the narrative.

---

## 🗡️ Act I: The Awakening

### Boss 1 — The Shade of Sloth
*First encounter: ~3 weeks from campaign start. Retuned to Haugh's profile.*

> From the mist between days, a shape forms. It wears your face on a lazy afternoon — the one where the book stayed closed and the guitar stayed in its case. It has waited a long time for you.

- **HP:** 600 (tuned above your current Lv 32 total)
- **Power Check:** Combined skill level total ≥ 40
- **Gate (both required):**
  - Complete **"The Unbroken Line"** quest — 7-day reading streak
  - **AND** complete **"The Refrain"** quest — 7 hours music in a 7-day window
- **Weakness:** Consistency (bonus damage at Lv 4+), Knowledge, Artistry
- **Reward:** **Morning's Edge** (weapon, Rare) — +5 Strength, +5 Consistency, +10% XP on days you log before noon
- **Trophy:** *First Shadow Slain* — cosmetic title **"The Awoken"** (replaces "The Half-Formed")

*The Shade is specifically shaped like your two weakest habits. This is the point. Beat it by closing the gap you already named.*

### Boss 2 — The Doomscroll Wyrm
*~Week 6*

> A slender dragon that feeds on wasted hours. Her scales are mirrors — every one shows something you almost watched.

- **HP:** 800
- **Power Check:** Combined levels ≥ 40, with Restraint ≥ Lv 4
- **Gate:** 14 consecutive days averaging <30 min of doomscrolling
- **Weakness:** Restraint, Wisdom
- **Reward:** **Mirror Shield** (relic, Rare) — 25% chance to reflect a Focus penalty back as XP
- **Trophy:** *Wyrmslayer* — unlocks Silent Citadel subtree bonus early

### Boss 3 — The Plateau Titan
*~Week 9*

> Twice your height, made of stone that's learned your patterns. He does not dodge. He just stands where you used to stop.

- **HP:** 1200
- **Power Check:** Combined levels ≥ 60
- **Gate:** Log a **personal best** on any habit — a heavier lift, a longer run, a harder piece of music, a longer focus block — and declare it.
- **Weakness:** Strength, Mastery
- **Reward:** **Titan's Core** (relic, Epic) — +10% XP on all habits, stacks with streaks
- **Trophy:** *Stonebreaker* — unlocks Act II

## How a boss fight plays out

When the Gate is open, Cowork will narrate the encounter. Expect something like:

> You find the Shade of Sloth in the hour before dawn. It yawns. "You again," it says. You roll initiative... (Cowork rolls your Strength + Resolve + weapon bonuses vs. the boss's Power Check.)

The fight resolves in one session — no multi-day grinding during the fight itself. The grind happens before, to meet the Gate.

**If you fail:** You survive (nothing is lost), but a *Regroup Quest* appears in your log. Complete it and you can re-challenge the boss.
