# Skill Trees

Four trees. Twelve skills. Every habit you log feeds one of them.

At certain skill levels, **subtrees** unlock — specializations that grant new gear drops or passive bonuses. Subtrees unlock automatically when their gate is met.

---

## ⚔️ BODY — "The Warrior's Path"

### Strength
Power from iron. Built in the gym, forged under load.
- **Lv 5:** Subtree unlocks — *Iron Discipline* (unlocks heavier gear slots)
- **Lv 10:** Unlocks boss fight stance *Juggernaut* (+20% dmg in boss fights)
- **Lv 20:** Unlocks title *Titan-Blooded*

Gear drops: Iron Gauntlets, Warrior's Pauldrons, Greatsword of Dawn

### Endurance
The long burn. Miles accumulated, breaths mastered.
- **Lv 5:** Subtree unlocks — *Second Wind* (HP regen +50%)
- **Lv 10:** Unlocks *Marathon Heart* (no HP loss on missed sleep up to 1 night/week)
- **Lv 20:** Unlocks title *The Unwearied*

Gear drops: Wayfarer's Boots, Cloak of the Road, Ranger's Bow

### Agility
Quickness, coordination, playful movement.
- **Lv 5:** Subtree unlocks — *Fleet-Footed* (+10% XP on any movement-based habit)
- **Lv 10:** Unlocks dodge mechanic (20% chance to negate boss HP damage)

Gear drops: Duelist's Rapier, Phantom Slippers

### Vitality
The body cared for. Fed, rested, maintained.
- **Lv 5:** Subtree unlocks — *Ironwood Constitution* (+30 Max HP)
- **Lv 10:** Unlocks *Dawn Warden* (wake-on-time grants +15% all-day XP mult)
- **Lv 20:** Unlocks title *The Everstanding*

Gear drops: Healer's Charm, Apothecary's Vest, Crystal of Rest

---

## 📖 MIND — "The Scholar's Path"

### Focus
Attention sustained. The rarest magic.
- **Lv 5:** Subtree unlocks — *Deep Current* (focus sessions over 90min grant +50% XP)
- **Lv 10:** Unlocks *Silent Citadel* (immune to one doomscroll penalty per week)
- **Lv 20:** Unlocks title *The Unshakable*

Gear drops: Scholar's Quill, Ring of Stillness, Hood of the Scribe

### Knowledge
Books consumed, concepts mastered.
- **Lv 5:** Subtree unlocks — *Library Built* (reading grants +1 MP per 10 minutes)
- **Lv 10:** Unlocks *Polymath* (cross-domain reading gains +25% Knowledge XP)
- **Lv 20:** Unlocks title *The Many-Tongued*

Gear drops: Student's Tome, Spectacles of Clarity, Staff of Pages

### Wisdom
The quiet mind. Seeing what is, not what you want.
- **Lv 5:** Subtree unlocks — *Inner Compass* (reflection grants foresight on boss weaknesses)
- **Lv 10:** Unlocks *Cold Clarity* (+1 Max MP per Wisdom level from here on)

Gear drops: Circlet of Clarity, Sage's Pendant, Mirror Shield

---

## 🎨 CRAFT — "The Artisan's Path"

### Artistry
Music, rhythm, voice, performance.
- **Lv 5:** Subtree unlocks — *True Voice* (Artistry sessions grant a Wisdom XP splash)
- **Lv 10:** Unlocks *Muse-Touched* (chance to generate an Inspiration item per practice)

Gear drops: Bard's Lute, Songweaver Sash, Resonant Ring

### Craft
Making things with hands — writing, art, cooking, building.
- **Lv 5:** Subtree unlocks — *Workshop* (your inventory gets +3 capacity)
- **Lv 10:** Unlocks *Maker's Eye* (crafted skill + Mastery XP synergy bonus)

Gear drops: Artisan's Apron, Woodcutter's Axe, Draftsman's Pen

### Mastery
Deliberate practice on a single hard skill. The grind-toward-excellence stat.
- **Lv 5:** Subtree unlocks — *Flow State* (Mastery sessions with no interruptions grant 2x XP)
- **Lv 10:** Unlocks *Apprentice → Journeyman* (title upgrade, cosmetic)
- **Lv 20:** Unlocks title *The Mastersmith*

Gear drops: Gloves of Precision, Focus Lens, Weighted Practice Blade

---

## 🛡️ DISCIPLINE — "The Monk's Path"

### Resolve
Doing the hard thing when no one's watching.
- **Lv 5:** Subtree unlocks — *Ironwill* (one doomscroll day per week is forgiven)
- **Lv 10:** Unlocks *Boss Slayer* (+15% damage to all bosses)
- **Lv 20:** Unlocks title *The Unshaken*

Gear drops: Monk's Sash, Ring of Oaths, Shield of the Steadfast

### Consistency
Showing up. The stat that compounds.
- **Lv 5:** Subtree unlocks — *Rhythm* (streaks add extra multiplier at 5/10/20 days)
- **Lv 10:** Unlocks *Sunrise Vow* (morning routine grants daily buff)

Gear drops: Timekeeper's Pendant, Boots of the Dawnwalker

### Restraint
Saying no. Not doing the thing. Harder than it sounds.
- **Lv 5:** Subtree unlocks — *No-Mind* (social media penalties halved)
- **Lv 10:** Unlocks *Fasting Blade* (optional hard-mode: gain bonus XP for declared abstentions)

Gear drops: Silent Mantle, Anchor Ring

## How gear drops work

When you hit a streak milestone or defeat a boss, Cowork rolls a drop from the related skill's pool. Drops scale in tier based on how hard the achievement was:

- 3-day streak → Common drop, low roll
- 7-day streak → Uncommon drop
- 14-day streak → Rare drop
- 30-day streak → Epic or Legendary drop
- Boss kill → Guaranteed drop at boss's tier + a story item
