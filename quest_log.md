## 📜 Active quests

### Main — "The Cut"
*Personal arc. The hero is lightening the load.*
- **Objective:** Maintain 130g protein / 1800 cal daily for the duration of the cut.
- **Duration:** ~4 weeks (declare end).
- **Reward on completion:** Epic relic **Apothecary's Crucible** (+15 Max HP, +10% Vitality XP permanent). Title upgrade available: "Forge-Lean."
- **Failure tolerance:** Up to 2 off-days per week without breaking the quest.

### Arc — "The Unbroken Line" (reading)
- **Objective:** 7 consecutive days of reading (20+ min each).
- **Reward:** Uncommon drop from Knowledge pool — likely **Bookmark of the Novice** or **Student's Tome**.
- **Why it matters:** This is one of two gates for Boss 1.

### Arc — "The Refrain" (music)
- **Objective:** 7 hours of music practice logged in a 7-day span (your stated weekly goal).
- **Reward:** Uncommon drop from Artistry pool — likely **Resonant Ring** or **Bard's Lute** (Common).
- **Why it matters:** This is the other gate for Boss 1.

### Arc — "Scrollbreaker"
- **Objective:** 7 consecutive days under 30 min of doomscrolling.
- **Reward:** Rare from Restraint pool — likely **Silent Mantle** (+8 Restraint, 20% chance to negate a Focus penalty).
- **Why it matters:** Direct fuel for your Focus stat and your MP pool. Also de-risks Boss 2 later.

### Arc — "Dawn Steps"
- **Objective:** Hit 10,000 steps on 5 of 7 days.
- **Reward:** Common **Wayfarer's Laces** (+1 Endurance, +2 Agility).
- **Progressive version:** Hit 10k on 20 of 30 days → Rare **Boots of the Dawnwalker**.

### Arc — "Iron Week"
- **Objective:** 4 gym sessions in one 7-day span (your target rhythm).
- **Reward:** Uncommon **Iron Bracers** (+5 Strength).
