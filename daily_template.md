## Minimum useful version

If that feels like a lot, here's the shortest thing that still runs the game:

```
Day [date]: gym __m, cardio __m, focus __h, read __m, music __m,
doomscroll __m, cals/protein __/__ , slept __h, steps __k,
[anything notable]
```

## What not to worry about

- **Precision.** 40ish min, ~5 hours, "about 30 mins" — all fine.
- **Listing zeros.** Skip anything you didn't do. I'll assume unlogged = not done.
- **Order.** Any order works.
- **Rest days.** Say "rest day today" and I'll protect your streaks (one per week without penalty).
