## Day 0 — Calibration

A hero does not appear from nothing. He is translated — measured, weighed, and written in.

Haugh of the Dawn stands at the threshold of the chronicle. He has already lifted iron. He has already turned pedals. He has already eaten with intention and slept enough to heal. He has also, in quieter hours, let his eyes drift too long across small glowing rectangles, and he has let books and instruments gather a thin layer of dust.

All of this is recorded. None of it is erased. He arrives at **Level 2** — not at zero. His starting gear is earned, not granted:
- The **Scholar's Tentative Hood**, for the hours he has already spent in focus.
- The **Dawn Sandals**, for the kilometers already cycled under dawn light.
- The **Apothecary's Token**, for the discipline of the weighed plate.

Ahead of him, in the mist of the third week, a shape is already forming. It wears his face. It has his unread books on its shelves. It is not yet named; it is only waiting.
